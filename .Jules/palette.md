## 2025-02-28 - Focus Visible Outlines in Dark Themes
**Learning:** Standard browser focus outlines are often invisible or look terrible against dark glassmorphism UI themes (like the zinc-900/90 used here).
**Action:** Always use explicit focus indicators that match the theme's accent color (e.g., `focus-visible:ring-2 focus-visible:ring-indigo-400 focus-visible:outline-none`) to ensure keyboard users can actually see what they are navigating on without breaking the aesthetic.
